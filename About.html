ABOUT US

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a user-friendly platform for collecting essential health information from individuals, ensuring the well-being of both the users and the PUP Community.

Form Development

We developed a comprehensive health declaration form to meet the needs of the PUP Organization and Community. Our form is designed to be secure, user-friendly, and adaptable to different use cases.

Collaboration and Feedback

Throughout the development process, we have engaged with healthcare professionals, students, and other stakeholders to gather feedback and ensure that our form meets the highest standards of accuracy and relevance.
